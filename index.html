1 Mission — Why this role exists
Own a blended portfolio of enterprise and mid-market customers through the complete post-sale lifecycle — from onboarding and value realization through renewal and expansion. The SCSM serves as a long-term strategic partner who guides customers through the change management required to deeply adopt CertifID's solutions, drives retention and growth, and connects product value to each customer's broader business outcomes.
